About
Son of Jomar and Cleid-Cler, Francisco Eduardo was born in Natal, RN Brazil, but he lives in Colorado, USA since 2000. He has been developing and teaching software and hardware design for the last 26 years in many countries, including Brazil, USA, Mexico, Japan, and England. He holds a BS is Computer Science with a minor in Electronics, a MS in Software Engineering, a MS in Computer Systems Security, and a Doctoral degree in Computer Science (Computer Science - Enterprise Systems Architecture). Francisco is also currently taking graduate-level classes in Data Sciences through Johns Hopkins University and taking Doctoral classes at Colorado Tech University.
